About Me

My Story

Welcome to my corner of the digital world! I'm a San Francisco-based UX designer with a background in the hospitality industry and now in UX design. What drew me to UX is its fusion of technology and human behavior, allowing me to craft not just visually appealing designs, but intuitive and engaging experiences for all users. My transition into UX was fueled by a genuine passion for creating impactful digital journeys that resonate with diverse audiences. I take pride in my ability to swiftly identify issues, brainstorm innovative solutions, and transform them into compelling designs. The evolving nature of UX excites me—I love the challenge of staying on top of trends and delivering user-centered experiences. Through this journey, I've honed a unique skill set—a blend of creativity, empathy, and problem-solving prowess. I'm eager to collaborate and bring your visionary ideas to life by infusing them with exceptional UX design. Let's embark on this creative journey together!
